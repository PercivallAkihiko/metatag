Receive and Watch the Video
Users receive videos from companies. Upon receiving them, they must watch them in their entirety to gain an understanding of their content.
Tagging Process
After watching the video, users engage in the tagging process by assigning relevant tags that best describe the content. Each user contributes to the categorization of the video based on their interpretation and understanding.
Voting and Rewards
Once all validators have tagged the video, the tagging results are revealed collectively. Users who agree with the majority consensus receive rewards for their contribution. However, users who dissent from the majority consensus face slashes. This incentivizes users to participate actively and align with the majority for optimal rewards.
